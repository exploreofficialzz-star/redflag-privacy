Overview

RedFlag Names ("the App") is operated by chAs Technologies LLC. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

By using RedFlag Names, you agree to the practices described in this policy. The app is intended for entertainment purposes only.

⚠️ RedFlag Names does not require account creation or login. We do not collect your name, email, or personal identity.
📊

Information We Collect

The App collects the following categories of data:

  • Names you enter — stored locally on your device only for history purposes. Never transmitted to our servers.
  • Advertising ID (GAID) — collected by Google AdMob to serve personalized ads to free users.
  • App usage data — collected anonymously by AdMob and Google Play to improve ad relevance.
  • Purchase records — if you purchase "Remove Ads Forever," Google Play processes the transaction. We only receive confirmation of the purchase status.
  • Device information — operating system version, device type, and app version, collected by AdMob for ad targeting.
📢

Advertising

RedFlag Names uses Google AdMob to display ads to free users. AdMob may collect and use your Advertising ID to show you personalized ads.

AdMob may collect:

  • Advertising ID (GAID on Android)
  • IP address and approximate location
  • App interaction data for ad measurement

You can opt out of personalized advertising in your device settings under Privacy → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization.

For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy.

👑 Users who purchase "Remove Ads Forever" ($2.99) will not be shown any ads and AdMob will not collect advertising data from those sessions.
💾

Local Data Storage

The following data is stored only on your device and is never transmitted to our servers:

  • Names you have analyzed (history)
  • Your app preferences and settings
  • Rewarded ad status (24-hour timer)
  • Purchase status for "Remove Ads Forever"

You can clear this data at any time by clearing the app's data from your device settings, or using the "Clear History" option inside the app.

🔔

Notifications

With your permission, RedFlag Names sends local push notifications to remind you to analyze names. These notifications are generated entirely on your device — no notification data is sent to external servers.

You can disable notifications at any time in your device's notification settings or inside the app.

💳

In-App Purchases

RedFlag Names offers a one-time in-app purchase: "Remove Ads Forever" ($2.99).

All payment processing is handled entirely by Google Play. We do not collect, store, or process your payment information. We only receive a confirmation that a purchase was completed.

For questions about billing, refer to Google Play Support.
